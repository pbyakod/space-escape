How to Play

Up: accelarate
Left: Rotate the ship anticlockwise
right: Rotate the ship clockwise
Space Bar: Shoot laser
You earn scores by hiting asteroids. 20 points for a large asteroids, 50 points for a medium asteroid, 100 points for a small asteroid
When a large asteroid is hit, it breaks into two medium asteroids
When a medium asteroid is hit, it breaks into two small asteroids
Asteroids move in random direction and velocity
Ship health decrease by 10 when crash against any asteroid
The game ends when your ship health hit 0 or all the asteroids are cleared.
